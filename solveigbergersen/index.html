Bio

@Lluis Calm Vidal

Solveig Bergersen is a Norwegian Soprano based in Sweden.

Having grown up on the windy west coast of Norway she is a wild and free spirit and this comes to show in her art expression and interpretations of roles and repertoire.

In june 2023 she graduated Stockholm University of the Arts Opera program, and performed the role of La Fee in Massenets Cendrillon as her examination at Folkoperan Stockholm. Former studies were conducted at the Norwegian Academy of Music, where she graduated a Bachelor of Classical Performance 2020.

She made her internship and debut at the Royal Swedish Opera as Papagena in Die Zauberflöte in fall season of 2022. She sang Amore in travelling summer shows of Gluck's «Orfeus and Eurydice» in the summer of 2022.

In the fall of 2023 Solveig looks forward to returning to the role of Papagena with Orkester Nord conducted by Martin Wåhlberg in Clermond-Ferrand and singing the Soprano solo in Bachs Weinachtsoratorium for the first time in Kristiansand Dome in december.

She is an experienced concert singer, with works such as Faure's Requiem, Mozart's Requiem and Mozart's Exultate Jubilate in the repertoire.
